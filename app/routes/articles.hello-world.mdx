Project Overview
The HealthTrack Mobile App is designed to help users take control of their health by offering comprehensive tracking and personalized recommendations. By leveraging advanced technology, the app provides insights into daily activities, health metrics, and reminders to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Key Features Implemented
Personalized Health Insights:

The app analyzes user data to provide tailored health insights and suggestions.
It offers daily summaries of health metrics such as steps taken, calories burned, and heart rate.
Activity Tracking:

Users can track their daily activities, including exercise routines and sedentary time.
The app provides visualizations of activity data, helping users stay motivated and informed about their progress.
Health Reminders:

The app includes a reminder system for medication, exercise, and other health-related tasks.
Users receive notifications to ensure they stay on track with their health goals.
User-Friendly Interface:

The app boasts a clean and modern design, making it easy to navigate and use.
Key sections such as Home, Insights, Reminders, and Profile are readily accessible through an intuitive navigation bar.
